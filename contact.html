Whether you have a laptop to donate, or you support disadvantaged young people who could use one, here's how to reach us.

For donors

How to donate

We're looking for good quality laptops in working order. Unfortunately we can't accept laptops that are:

  • More than 10 years old
  • Damaged — smashed screens or other physical damage

I'm afraid we can't accept desktop computers, tablets, or mobile phones.

Single laptop donations

Horley Library is our drop-off location for single laptop donations. There's no need to contact the library in advance — just head to the reception desk and mention the laptop is for Level Up.

Multiple or business donations

To donate multiple laptops, please get in touch:

info@leveluplaptops.org.uk

For organisations

How to request laptops

Level Up exclusively donates laptops via trusted charities, schools and government organisations. I'm sorry to say we don't donate laptops directly to individuals.

If you represent an organisation that helps disadvantaged young people in the East Surrey region and you need laptops, please get in touch:

info@leveluplaptops.org.uk
