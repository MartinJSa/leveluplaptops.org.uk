STEM & Gaming Cafe

Twice a year we run a STEM and gaming cafe for young people at the Horley Health Hub. The purpose of the cafe is to bring teenagers in with the draw of computer games and then introduce them to STEM topics such as 3D design and printing, coding and robotics.
