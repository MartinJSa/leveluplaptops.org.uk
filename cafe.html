STEM & Gaming Cafe

Twice a year we run a STEM and gaming cafe for young people at the Horley Health Hub. The idea is simple: bring teenagers in with the draw of computer games, then introduce them to STEM topics — 3D design and printing, coding, and robotics.

A charitable project founded and run by Martin Saunders in Horley, Surrey. Securely refurbishing donated laptops for disadvantaged young people across Surrey and Sussex.
